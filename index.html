Moles!

This is not the kinda mole we mean.




A mole is a unit of measurement, the base unit in Science to measure things in terms of atoms without using ridiculous numbers of atoms and not understanding what's happening. It also makes math much simpler in terms of doing math with minuscule chemistry-related things.





Moles to Atoms Formula

To calculate the number of atoms from moles, multiply the number of moles by 6.0221415*10^23



Atoms to Moles Formula

To calculate the number of moles from atoms, divide the number of atoms by 6.0221415*10^23
